
Do's: 

Set all personal profiles to "Private" to control your digital footprint.

Verify the source of news or articles before sharing them.

Use professional language when interacting with academic or industry pages.

Credit the original creator when sharing images or designs.

Use a strong, unique password for every social account.


Don'ts:

Don't post sensitive personal information like your home address or phone number.

Don't engage in or support cyberbullying or online arguments.

Don't click on suspicious links sent through direct messages.

Don't post content that could be viewed as unprofessional by future employers.

Don't share your login credentials with anyone, even friends.
