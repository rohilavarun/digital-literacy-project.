Use MFA: Always enable Multi-Factor Authentication on your college and banking accounts.

Verify Links: Never click on "urgent" links in emails; instead, go directly to the official website.

Update Software: Keep your OS and antivirus updated to patch security "holes."

Public Wi-Fi: Avoid logging into sensitive accounts while on public or open Wi-Fi networks.

Backup Data: Regularly save your project files to a secure cloud or external drive.
